PS5 review: PS5 is still the best console available now, even well into 2024 

 Posted by Nithma Fernando (12.06.2023)

The PS5 was announced in 2019 as the successor to the PS4 and it was released on 12th November 2020. The PS5 was introduced in the sane month as Microsoft' s xbox serious X and serious S console , forming the 9th generation of video game consoles. The PS5 carries 2 models as the Base edition and Digital edition. 

 

The dimensions of Base edition is about 390 × 260 × 104mm and the digital edition is about 390 × 260 × 92mm and weigh 4.5KG and 3.9KG in order. With an HDMI 2.1 input , the PS5 can play 4K cotent at 120 frames per second and while HDMI 2.1 monitors are becoming more common , you can still buy 4K quality HDMI 2.0 monitors for less money. 

The PS5 has 825GB of storage space , but only 667.2GB of useable memory to play with (a considerable portion of which is allocated for system data and the playstation 's operating system) and the majority of new PS5 games weighing in at 50GB space will be limited.

 

The PS5 is equipped with 16GB of GDDR6 RAM. Any gaming console 's RAM is an active and crucial component , and it 's great that playstation has included the latest version , which has enhanced capacity and bandwidth.Although the PS5 doesn't support all file types, it does handle the most prevalent ones, such as MP4 , MP3 and MKV. It 's worth mentioning that the PS5 can play all of the same file types as the PS4.  

PS5 creates some of the most advanced 3D audio accessible with object - based spatial sound techno. The controller of PS5 can be used both wired and wireless.

PLAY HAS NO LIMITS  

 

 
  • Power and Performance: The PS5 is powered by a custom AMD Zen 2 CPU and an RDNA 2-based GPU, offering improved graphics and faster load times compared to its predecessor. It also supports ray tracing, 4K resolution, and up to 120 frames per second (fps) gameplay. 
  • Ultra-High-Speed SSD: The PS5 features a built-in solid-state drive (SSD) that significantly reduces loading times, allowing for faster game installations and seamless transitions between game levels.
  • DualSense Controller: The PS5 introduced a new controller called the DualSense. It features adaptive triggers, haptic feedback, and an improved built-in microphone. These features provide more immersive gameplay experiences by offering realistic tactile sensations and responsive controls.
  • User Interface and User Experience: The PS5 features an updated user interface (UI) that focuses on a more intuitive and user-friendly experience. It provides quick access to key features and social interactions, making it easier for players to connect with friends, access content, and discover new games.
  •  PlayStation Plus Collection: PlayStation Plus subscribers have access to the PlayStation Plus Collection, a curated library of popular PlayStation 4 games available to download and play on the PS5 at no additional cost.

    The PS5' S main hardware features include a solid state drive optimized for high speed data, streaming, an AMD GPU capable of 4K resolution display at upto 120 frames persecond, hardware - accelerated ray tracing for realistic lighting and reflections and the tempest engine, which enables hardware accelerated 3D audio effects.
