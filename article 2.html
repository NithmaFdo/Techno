Apple AirPods 3 review: solid revamp with better fit and longer battery 

 Posted by Nithma Fernando (12.06.2023)

The 3rd generation Airpods (airpods 3) were unvieled during Apple's October launch event. Airpods (each) has 30.79mm Height 18.26mm width and 19.21mm Depth and it weighs 4.28g. Airpod 3 's Magsafe charging case has 46.4mm Height , 54.4mm width 21.38mm Depth and it weighs 37.91g. Airpods 3 has H1 headphone chip and it also has a quality that resist water and sweat.  By pushing the force sensor on the stem , you may operate your Airpods 3. The force sensor is programmed to conduct the following automatically. Audio can be played and paused in the following ways. One press to play , pause or answer a call , double press to skip forward , triple press to skip back , press and hold for get siri and say 'Hey siri' to do things like play a song or get direction.

 

Apple airpods 3 have a more compact stem , revised contoured fit, adaptive EQ and spatial audio support and more. Bluetooth 5.0 is the connectivity of airpods 3. Apple airpods 3 have some special sensors such as dual beamforming microphone , Inward facing microphone , Skin detect sensor , motion and speech detecting accelerometer and force sensor. The features of airpods 3 the first airpods charging case that is water resistant. The Magsafe charging case for the 3rd generation airpods is making it the first charging case with an IPX 4 rating for the standard airpods. 

 

 

Each airpods works up to 6 hours of listening time with a single charge and upto 4 hours of talk time with a single charge. And also with Magsafe charging case it is upto 30 hours of listening time and upto 20 hours of talk time with a single charge . There are airpods , Magsafe charging case , Lighting to USB -C cable and docummentation include in the box. People with impairment can make the most of their new airpods thank to accessibility features.And the airpods third generation's system requirements are iphone and ipod with the latest version of iOS, ipad with the latest version of ipadOS, Apple watch with latest version of watchOS, MAC models with the latest version of MacOS and Apple TV models with the latest tvOS. 

Audio Sharing. Audio Sharing allows for convenient sharing of songs or shows between any two pairs of AirPods. By bringing the AirPods in close proximity to the iPhone, iPad, or Apple TV that is playing the audio, they can be instantly connected and synced, enabling a seamless sharing experience.

 

 

 

 

 

Enhanced Find My. With the enhanced Find My feature, locating your AirPods becomes easier than ever. Utilizing the Find My app, you can track down your AirPods effortlessly. The proximity view allows you to determine their proximity, while separation alerts notify you when they go out of Bluetooth range.
