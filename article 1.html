Apple reveals the iPhone 13 and iPhone 13 Pro range 

 Posted by Nithma Fernando (12.06.2023)

Apple. inc designed and released the iphone 13 pro max.It's the 15th generation iphone's flagship smartphones, succeeding the iphone 12 pro and iphone 12 pro max. On September 14th 2021 , the devices were introduced alongside the iphone 13 and iphone 13 mini during an apple special event in Cupertino, Calirfornia. Silver, Graphite, Gold, Sierra Blue and Alpine Green are the five colors available for the iphone 13 pro max. Sierra Blue is a color that has taken the place of Paccific Blue. Apple announed a new Alpine Green color option at it's special event on March, 2022. 

 

This latest iphone has 160.8mm Height, 78.1mm Width and 7.65mm Depth. It weighs 240g. It includes original ios 15.0 and now it is ios 15.4.1. The iphone 13 pro max has 6.7 inch display with 1284 Ã— 2778 pixels. It includes a super retina XDR OLED display with improved normal brightness upto 1000 nits and peak brightness upto 1200 nits , as well as a variable 10 - 120Hz promotion display for the 1st time in an iphone, which can even go as low as 10Hz to save battery life. The A15 Bionic processor in the iphone 13 pro max is built by Apple and has a 16 - core CPU and 5 - core GPU .

 

 

 

 

The iphone 13 pro max has four cameras, one on the front and three on the back, including a telephoto, wide angle and ultra wide camera. All of the back facing cameras have bigger sensors than the iphone 12 series, enabling for more light to be collected. Larger apertures on the wide and ultra wide lenses capture more light and improve low low light performance. For the 1st time, the ultra wide camera incorporate auto focus.  

The 77mm telephoto lens has a narrower apertune than the 12 pro max but it has the advantage of being the first camera to support night mode. The bigger telephoto lens also boosts the digital zoom to 15x. The design of the iphone 13 pro and and iphone pro max is mostly unaltered from that of it's prodecessor. 

The iphone 13 pro max has upto 2.5 hours more battery life than its predecessor. The 13 pro max battery has a capacity of 4352mAh. It supports magsafe charging upto 15W , Qi wireless charging upto 7.5W and lighting charging upto 20-27 W. Only the iphone , USB type C to lighting cable , Documentation , sim ejector tool and the apple sticker are in the box of iphone 13 pro max.
